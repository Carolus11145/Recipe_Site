Black Eyed Pea Soup

A Quick Look At The Dish

This dish is a delectable soup perfect for vegetarians. It's main ingredients are primarily vegetables with the titular legumes (the black eyed peas). There are also the optional macaroni noodles and Parmesan cheese, but they may be omitted if you wish to keep this quick dish gluten-free and vegan.

*Interesting fact => The classic version of this soup was eaten in the American South during the Civil War for good luck.

Ingredients you will need:

  • 1 tbsp, vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 chopped carrot
  • 2 ribs of celery
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cups vegetable juice
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 16 oz tomatoes
  • 1/4 tsp, basil
  • 1/4 tsp, oregano
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 can black-eyed peas
  • 1/2 cup macaroni (optional)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Preparing the soup

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a heavy saucepan.
  2. Add the garlic, carrot, celery and onion and saute for 5 minutes.
  3. Reduce the heat and add the vegetable juice, vegetable broth, tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, pepper and black-eyed peas.
  4. Bring to boil then reduce to a simmer, and heat for ten minutes.
  5. (Optional) Add macaroni and heat for an additional 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally until macaroni is cooked.
