Black Eyed Pea Soup

A Quick Look At The Dish

This dish is a delectable soup perfect for vegetarians. It's main ingredients are primarily vegetables with the titular legumes (the black eyed peas). There are also the optional macaroni noodles and Parmesan cheese, but they may be omitted if you wish to keep this quick dish gluten-free and vegan.

*Interesting fact => The classic version of this soup was eaten in the American South during the Civil War for good luck.

  • 1 tbsp, vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 chopped carrot
  • 2 ribs of celery
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cups vegetable juice
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 16 oz tomatoes
  • 1/4 tsp, basil
  • 1/4 tsp, oregano
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 can black-eyed peas
  • 1/2 cup macaroni (optional)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)
